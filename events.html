Upcoming Events

Join us for creative workshops, community gatherings, and delightful experiences that bring people together over great coffee and beautiful moments.

Bloom & Tune Nights

Every Friday at 7 PM, let your soul bloom to the sound of local acoustic artists while sipping your favorite breew. Perfect for slow evenings, chill dates, or just soaking in the cozy cafe vibes.

Bloom Barista Academy

Monthly Saturday mornings, join our hands-on latte art workshop and learn to create beautiful designs - hearts, tulips. and rosettas - with our baristas guiding you every step of the way.

Sip & Paint Saturdays

Every 2nd Saturday at 6 PM, let your creativity flow! We'll provide the canvas, paints, and brushes - you just bring your imagination (and enjoy your coffee while painting!)

Brew & Play Nights

Every last Wednesday of the month, gather your friends for a night of board games, card games, and laughter. Complimentary cookie with every drink purchase for that extra cozy touch.

Bloom Market Days

Every 3rd Sunday of the month, support local artists and makers! Shop unique art prints, stickers, keychains, and more - all while sipping your favorite brew and enjoying live demos from creators.

Building Community Through Coffee

At Brew & Bloom, we believe in the power of bringing people together. Our events are designed to foster connections, inspire creativity, and create lasting memories. Whether you're a regular or visiting for the first time, you'll find a warm welcome and a sense of belonging in our botanical haven.
